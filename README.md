## 1. Customer (User)
Users are the people who buy food.

### Account Management
- **Register:** Create a new account to use the system.
- **Log In:** Access the account to order food.
- **Log Out:** Sign out to keep the account safe.
- **Update Profile:** Change personal details if needed.

### Food Ordering
- **View Menu:** See all available food items.
- **Search Food:** Find specific food quickly.
- **View Details:** See prices and descriptions before ordering.
- **Add to Cart:** Select food to buy.
- **Change Quantity:** Choose how many of each item to buy.
- **Remove from Cart:** Take items out if you change your mind.
- **Place Order:** Finalize the purchase.
- **Select Pickup Time:** Choose when to collect the food.

### Order Tracking
- **View Status:** Check if the food is ready.
- **Get Notifications:** Get updates about the order.
- **View History:** See a list of past orders.

---

## 2. Admin
Admins manage the daily canteen operations.

### Food Management
- **Request Changes:** Ask the Super Admin to add or update food items.
- **Edit Info:** Update food details like descriptions.
- **Delete Items:** Remove items that are no longer offered.
- **Update Prices:** Keep prices up to date.
- **Availability:** Mark items as "Ready" or "Sold Out."

### Order Management
- **View All Orders:** See what people have ordered.
- **Update Status:** Change status (e.g., "In Progress," "Ready").
- **Cancel Orders:** Remove orders that cannot be fulfilled.

### Customer Management
- **View Users:** See who is using the system.
- **Report Accounts:** Request the Super Admin to block suspicious users.

### Reports
- **Daily Sales:** Check how much food was sold each day.
- **Popular Food:** See which items customers like the most.

---

## 3. Super Admin
The Super Admin manages the system and the admins.

### Admin Management
- **Create Admins:** Add new staff accounts.
- **Edit Admins:** Update admin account info.
- **Deactivate Admins:** Stop access for old or unauthorized staff.

### System Management
- **Manage Roles:** Control what admins and users can do.
- **View Activities:** Monitor what is happening in the system.
- **Settings:** Change system configurations.
- **Backup Data:** Save a copy of the data.
- **Restore Data:** Recover data if something goes wrong.

### Reports and Monitoring
- **View Reports:** See all system and sales reports.
- **Sales Statistics:** See overall business performance.
- **Monitor Admins:** Check that admins are doing their jobs correctly.
